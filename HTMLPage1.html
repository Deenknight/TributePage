Charlamagne the Great

Leader of men, creator of empires

A man of great accomplishments

  • 742-747 - Presumably born near Aachen or Liege
  • 768 - Became the king of the Franks, along with his brother Carloman
  • 771 - Inherited his brother's titles after he died of natural causes
  • 772-799 - Conquers the territories around him and consolidates power
  • 800 - Declared roman empire by Pope Leo III
